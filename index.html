How Latitude Relates to Climate Conditions

July 31, 2022 earth image courtesy of JPL/NASA

The geographic locations used in this data analysis were randomly generated on July 31, 2022. They were converted to latitude, longitude. From the coordinates, climate data was pulled from the Open Weather Website, and jsonified. Its now cleaned it up, and saved it to a cvs file. You can navigate to visualizations from that data from the "visualizations" drop down tab above. You will also find links to the raw data, or a comparison of all the plots.
